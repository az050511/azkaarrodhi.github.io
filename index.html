Welcome to My Website!

Hello, my name is Azka, an 8th-grade middle school student with a keen interest in web design and playing chess. Both hobbies provide me with opportunities to think creatively and strategically.

How My Hobbies Began

My interest in web design started when I saw how websites are built and function. I began by learning HTML to create the basic structure of web pages, then moved on to CSS to enhance their appearance, and finally delved into JavaScript to make the sites more interactive.

In addition, I also have a passion for chess. I started by learning the basic rules and common openings. Then, I deepened my knowledge of tactics and strategies such as forks and checkmating, as well as how to plan my moves to achieve victory.

Using AI in Web Design

In web design, I often use AI to help solve complex coding issues. AI is incredibly useful when I encounter hard-to-find bugs or need to expedite the development process with efficient solutions.

Although I am still in the learning process, every small step in both hobbies helps me grow and improve.
